// Our Services //

Rain or shine, you’re protected.


WetShield Waterproofing has been the leading name in waterproofing solutions, offering comprehensive services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our team of experienced professionals utilizes cutting-edge technologies and industry-leading products to combat every water intrusion challenge.

ROOF WATERPROOFING

Shield your roof from leaks and weather damage.

Roof waterproofing is a protective layer applied to a roof to prevent water from entering the home and causing damage. Roof waterproofing can also help protect the building's longevity.

BASEMENT SEALING

Prevent water seepage and mold growth in basements.

Basement sealing involves applying a moisture barrier to walls and floors to prevent water seepage. It helps avoid mold growth, protects structural integrity, and ensures a dry, usable space.

BATHROOM & KITCHEN WATERPROOFING

Protect wet areas from moisture-related issues.

Bathroom and kitchen waterproofing protects wet areas from leaks and moisture damage. It prevents mold growth, safeguards surfaces, and extends the life of tiles, walls, and cabinetry in these spaces.

TERRACE & BALCONY WATERPROOFING

Ensure outdoor spaces are water-resistant.

Terrace and balcony waterproofing involves applying a protective layer to prevent water penetration. It protects outdoor spaces from rain damage, leaks, and structural deterioration, ensuring long-lasting durability and safety.

EXPANSION JOINT TREATMENT

Seal joints to prevent water ingress.

Expansion joint treatment involves sealing gaps between building sections to prevent water ingress. It ensures flexibility, accommodates structural movement, and protects against leaks, cracks, and long-term water-related damage.

CRACK INJECTION

Repair and seal structural cracks effectively

Crack Injection is a technique used to fill and seal structural cracks in concrete and masonry structures. It prevents water leakage, restores structural integrity, and protects the building from further damage caused by moisture and environmental factors.

// Frequently Asked Questions //

What types of surfaces can WetShield Waterproofing be applied to?

WetShield is suitable for a wide range of surfaces including concrete roofs, terraces, basements, exterior walls, water tanks, and bathrooms. It provides a strong, flexible, and durable waterproof barrier on both horizontal and vertical surfaces.

How long does WetShield Waterproofing last once applied?

When applied correctly, WetShield Waterproofing can last up to 8–10 years, depending on surface condition, climate, and maintenance. Regular inspections and timely touch-ups can help extend its lifespan even further.

Is WetShield Waterproofing resistant to UV rays and weather conditions?

Yes, WetShield is UV-resistant and designed to withstand extreme weather conditions including heat, rain, and humidity. It forms an elastic membrane that expands and contracts with temperature changes, making it ideal for outdoor applications.
